About Me

Athanasios was born and raised in Thessaloniki, a lovely city by the sea in the north of Greece where people are famous for their easiness and openness.

He then moved to Volos a beautiful city in the center of Greece that was the birthplace of Thiseas, in the shade of mountain Pilio - home of Centaures, to study Computer & Communication Engineering.

Before finishing his studies, he moved to Geneva in Switzerland, a multicultural city in the borders of France, to work at his MSc Thesis at CERN.

He is still living in Geneva while working at CERN.
